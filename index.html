Gödel Prime Encoder

Transform text into numbers and binary pixel art

How it works

The Gödel prime encoding converts text to a unique number:

N = p1c1 × p2c2 × p3c3 × ...

Where pi is the i-th prime and ci is the character value:

  • A-Z → 1-26
  • 0-9 → 27-36
